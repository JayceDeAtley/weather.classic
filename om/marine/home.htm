Dissemination Information

  • INTERNET Read me!
    Marine weather products via the Internet     
  • NOAA WEATHER RADIO 
    Voice broadcasts of coastal marine forecasts on a continuous cycle
  • USCG HF RADIOFAX
    Broadcast of high seas weather maps     
  • WWV, WWVH HF VOICE (TIME TICK)
    Voice broadcast of high seas warnings 
  • USCG HF SITOR (NBDP)
    Broadcast of high seas and offshore forecasts in text form. 
  • USCG HF VOICE:
    Broadcast of high seas forecasts and storm warnings via single sideband (SSB) radio
  • USCG MF VOICE:
    Broadcast of offshore forecasts and storm warnings via single sideband (SSB) radio
  • USCG VHF VOICE: 
    Broadcast of coastal forecasts and storm warnings     
  • USCG AIS BROADCAST: 
    Broadcast of Observations via the Automatic Identification System (AIS)     
  • COMMERCIAL MARITIME COAST STATIONS and WEATHER NETS 
    Weather broadcasts from commercial coast stations and weather nets
  • COMMERCIAL SERVICES PROVIDING MARINE FORECASTS VIA SATELLITE
    Weather broadcasts from commercial satellite services
  • AMATEUR "HAM" RADIO  
    Marine forecasts made available by radio amateurs for the small boat and yachting communities. 
  • ALASKA MARINE VHF VOICE 
    NOAA broadcast of offshore forecasts, coastal forecasts, and storm warnings for the state of Alaska
  • ALASKA MARINE HF VOICE - 4125 kHz 
    NOAA broadcast of offshore forecasts, coastal forecasts, and storm warnings for the state of Alaska 
  • COASTAL WARNING DISPLAY PROGRAM 
    Visual signals to alert mariners of storms at sea
  • GMDSS 
    Provides emergency and safety communications and disseminates Maritime Safety Information 
  • NAVTEX 
    USCG broadcast of offshore forecasts and storm warnings in text form 
  • INMARSAT-C SafetyNET 
    Broadcast of high seas forecasts and warnings via satellite
  • SPACE WEATHER
    Sunspots and other phenomena affecting radio and satellite reception     
  • PORTS 
    Real-time information that supports safe and cost-efficient navigation
  • EMWIN 
    Suite of methods of making available a live data stream of basic weather data
  • NOAA WEATHER WIRE SERVICE  
    Primary telecommunications network for NWS forecasts, warnings, and other products to the mass media, emergency management agencies, and private weather services 
  • FAMILY OF SERVICES 
    Composed of multiple data streams of meteorological and hydrological messages, it serves mass media, emergency management agencies, and private weather services
  • NOAAPORT 
    Satellite broadcast of NWS forecasts, warnings, and other products to the mass media, emergency management agencies, and private weather services 
  • NEXRAD Radar Product Central Collection/Distribution Service (RPCCDS)  
    Real-time weather radar images 
  • NOAA WEATHER SATELLITES  
    NOAA geostationary and polar-orbiting satellites 
  • MASS MEDIA 
    Commercial radio, TV, and other media 
  • PRIVATE WEATHER SERVICES PROVIDERS  
    Value-added and unique weather products from private weather services 
  • NOAA TELEPHONE RECORDINGS 
    Recorded marine forecasts , Dial-A-Buoy and PORTS. 
  • PUBLICATIONS 
    Marine Weather Services maps and other publications 
  • PHONE NUMBERS and ADDRESSES  
    Contacts for questions or comments
